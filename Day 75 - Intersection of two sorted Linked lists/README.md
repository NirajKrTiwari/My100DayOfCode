Intersection of two sorted Linked lists
----------------------------------------
Testcase 1:

Input:
L1 = 1->2->3->4->6
L2 = 2->4->6->8
Output: 2 4 6
Explanation: For the given first two
linked list, 2, 4 and 6 are the elements
in the intersection. 

Testcase 2:

Input:
L1 = 10->20->40->50
L2 = 15->40
Output: 40
